# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive experience for everyone who contributes to PerpsTrader, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Be respectful.** Treat others with dignity and kindness. Disagreements are fine — personal attacks are not.

**Be constructive.** Give thoughtful feedback. Critique code, not people.

**Be inclusive.** Use welcoming language. Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.

**Be collaborative.** Listen before you respond. Ask questions when you're unsure.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or personal insults
- Discrimination based on race, gender, orientation, disability, religion, or any other characteristic
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Deliberate intimidation or unwelcome sexual attention
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the maintainers privately. All reports will be handled confidentially and investigated promptly.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove, block, or ban anyone who violates this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, code reviews, and any community channels associated with PerpsTrader.
